How to make Lumpiang Shanghai

ingredients:

  • 500 grams (1 lb) ground pork or beef
  • 1 medium-sized carrot, finely chopped or grated
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 50 lumpia wrappers (spring roll wrappers)
  • Cooking oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Filling:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground pork (or beef), chopped carrots, onion, and minced garlic.
    • Add the egg, soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), salt, and pepper.
    • Mix all the ingredients thoroughly until well combined. You can use your hands or a spoon to ensure the mixture is evenly seasoned.
  2. Wrap the Lumpia:
    • Separate the lumpia wrappers carefully to avoid tearing them.
    • Place one wrapper on a clean, flat surface with one corner pointing towards you (diamond shape).
    • Spoon about 1 to 1 Â½ tablespoons of the filling near the bottom corner of the wrapper, shaping it into a small log.
    • Fold the bottom corner of the wrapper over the filling, then fold the sides inward.
    • Roll the wrapper tightly towards the top corner to enclose the filling completely.
    • Seal the edge of the wrapper with a little water or beaten egg to keep it from unwrapping during frying.
    • Repeat the process until all the filling is used.
  3. Fry the Lumpia:
    • Heat cooking oil in a deep pan or wok over medium-high heat.
    • Once the oil is hot, carefully place the lumpia rolls into the oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pan.
    • Fry the lumpia in batches, turning them occasionally, until they are golden brown and crispy on all sides. This usually takes about 3-5 minutes per batch.
    • Remove the fried lumpia and drain on a paper towel-lined plate to remove excess oil.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve the Lumpiang Shanghai hot with your choice of dipping sauce. Popular options include sweet and sour sauce, banana ketchup, or a vinegar-based dip with garlic and chili.

Enjoy your delicious, homemade Lumpiang Shanghai!
