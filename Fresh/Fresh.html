How to make Fresh Lumpia

ingredients:

For the Filling:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup julienned carrots
  • 1/2 cup julienned green beans
  • 1/2 cup tofu, cubed and lightly fried (optional)
  • 1/2 cup cooked and chopped shrimp or chicken (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sauce:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Filling:
    • Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
    • Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
    • Add the tofu (if using) and cook until lightly browned.
    • Add the vegetables and stir-fry for about 5 minutes, or until they are tender but still crisp.
    • Add soy sauce, sugar, and salt and pepper to taste. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and let cool.
  2. Make the Sauce:
    • In a saucepan, combine water, soy sauce, sugar, and minced garlic. Heat over medium heat.
    • In a small bowl, dissolve cornstarch in a little bit of water and add it to the saucepan.
    • Stir constantly until the sauce thickens. Remove from heat and let cool. Add vinegar if desired.
  3. Assemble the Fresh Lumpia:
    • Place a wrapper on a clean surface.
    • Spoon some of the filling onto the center of the wrapper.
    • Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling and then roll up from the bottom to enclose the filling.
    • Repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling.
  4. Serve:
    • Arrange the fresh lumpia on a serving plate.
    • Drizzle with the sauce and sprinkle with chopped peanuts.

Enjoy your delicious, homemade Fresh Lumpia!
