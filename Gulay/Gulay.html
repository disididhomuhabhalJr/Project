How to make Lumpiang Gulay

ingredients:

For the Filling:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1 cup carrots, finely shredded
  • 1 cup green beans, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup bean sprouts (optional)
  • 1 medium potato, peeled and diced (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Lumpia Wrappers:

  • Lumpia wrappers (available at Asian stores or you can use spring roll wrappers)
  • 1 egg, beaten (for sealing the wrappers)

For Frying:

  • Vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Filling:
    • Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
    • Saut√© the chopped onion and minced garlic until they become translucent.
    • Add the diced potato (if using) and cook until tender.
    • Add the shredded cabbage, carrots, and green beans to the pan. Stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
    • Add the bean sprouts (if using) and soy sauce. Stir well and cook for another 2 minutes.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and let the filling cool.
  2. Assemble the Lumpia:
    • Place a lumpia wrapper on a clean surface, with one corner facing you (like a diamond shape).
    • Spoon about 2-3 tablespoons of the vegetable filling onto the lower part of the wrapper.
    • Fold the bottom corner over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll up tightly, sealing the top corner with a bit of beaten egg to ensure it stays closed.
    • Repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling.
  3. Fry the Lumpia:
    • Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Fry the lumpia in batches, being careful not to overcrowd the pan, until they are golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side.
    • Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve hot with your choice of dipping sauce, such as vinegar with garlic and chili or a sweet and sour sauce.

Enjoy your delicious, homemade Lumpiang Gulay!
